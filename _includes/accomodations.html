Accommodations

If you are coming from out of town, we recommend staying at the Half Moon Bay Inn (Evan and Christina will be staying there). If that is not your cup of tea, then we suggest searching for hotels/AirBnBs in the Pescadero or Half Moon Bay areas.

While the city of San Francisco seems nearby, there is often a lot of traffic on route 280/90 and route 1. You will probably end up saving a lot of time staying in the area... and it is really cute!
